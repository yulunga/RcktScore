Beta Access & Early Access

HitnScore is currently in active development and preparing for its first public release.

We’re building a modern scoring platform designed specifically for racket sports, starting with squash and expanding into tennis, padel, badminton, pickleball and more.

During this early stage, access to the platform is intentionally limited while we continue refining the experience, improving performance and shaping the future direction of the app with real player and club feedback.

Beta Access gives selected users an opportunity to explore HitnScore before public release.

  • Preview versions of the scoring platform
  • Early feature testing
  • New match and scoring experiences
  • Experimental functionality before launch
  • Opportunities to help shape future development

As the platform evolves, features and availability may change regularly during the beta phase.

Why is access limited?

We want to ensure the best possible experience as HitnScore develops.

  • Improve reliability and performance
  • Gather real-world feedback from players and clubs
  • Fine-tune the user experience
  • Prioritise the features that matter most
  • Build a stable and scalable platform for wider release

HitnScore is being designed for:

  • Individual players
  • Clubs and venues
  • Coaches
  • Leagues and competitions
  • Social and competitive racket sports communities

Whether it’s a casual club night or competitive match play, the goal is simple:

Make scoring smarter, faster and more connected.
